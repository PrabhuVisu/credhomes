Cred Homes Commandments

Good Credit Score

You must have a credit score of 750+ to be able to rent a house listed on Cred Homes. Being a match maker, our job is to list best properties for tenants and get the best tenants for owners.

Digital Payments Only

We believe in comfort for all, hence every payment you make is through digital channels only - Netbanking, Credit/Debit Cards, Wallets, UPI - whatever you like. No CASH!

Pay On Time

We need you to setup standing instructions for automatic rent payments. You must ensure any dues are paid on time

Build Your Reputation
